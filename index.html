Event Overview: Navrang 5.0

The Microtek Greenburg Cultural Committee proudly invites you to partner with us for Navrang 5.0, our annual Navratri festival. This two-day cultural extravaganza, held on September 27th and 28th, 2025, is more than just an event; it's a celebration that unites our community of over 2000 residents through music, dance, and cultural performances.

Navrang 5.0 provides a vibrant platform for businesses to connect with a premium, affluent, and engaged audience. Our residents are active consumers who trust local recommendations and appreciate businesses that support their community. By sponsoring Navrang 5.0, you will directly engage with your target market, build brand loyalty, and become a part of the rich cultural tapestry of Microtek Greenburg.

Event Highlights & Audience Demographics

Microtek Greenburg is a top-tier residential society in New Gurgaon with over 2000 residents from diverse age groups. Our events consistently attract a footfall of approximately 1500 people over two days, making them an ideal environment for your brand to be seen and remembered. Our audience is primarily composed of families with children, young professionals, and senior citizens, all of whom have high spending capacity and are active and engaged consumers.

Sponsoring our event is an investment in direct brand exposure and community goodwill. Unlike mass-market advertising, this opportunity allows you to create a personal connection with potential customers right in their own neighborhood. The society's strong word-of-mouth and recommendation system is a powerful driver of business for our sponsors.
